Welcome to Constellation

Constellation manages Planets.

  • You can create new Planets through the web, attached to groups.
  • You can manage the set of feeds for each Planet through the web.
  • You can comment on individual items that pass through a Planet's river.
  • You can archive links from the items that pass through a Planet's river.
